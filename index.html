Mother Earth

Mother Earth is carved with wonders and beauty.
As you wake up in the morning and step into the bright light, you walk to sit under a tree and gaze at the sunrise.


The view is so beautiful. As the sunshine grows hotter, you seek shelter. At the end of the day, you return to watch the sun go down and evening falls.

You lay down on your back, with a perfect point of view, staring at the somewhat dark sky adorned with beautiful little stars, accompanied by the moon in between. It is indeed a wonderful world.
